> Converters: `gml2gv`, `gv2gml`, `gv2gxl`, `gxl2gv`, `graphml2gv` & `mm2gv`.

`gv2gxl -o {{output.gxl}} {{input.gv}}`

- Convert a graph using `stdin` and `stdout`:
